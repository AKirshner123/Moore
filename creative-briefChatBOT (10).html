🌟 Make-A-Wish (Demo)
Campaign Overview
This campaign celebrates a historic milestone in Make-A-Wish history: the 650th wish granted by John Cena — the most by any individual in the foundation's 40+ year existence. A 3-minute hero video package will anchor the campaign, honoring Cena's unbroken 20-year commitment to critically ill children and the transformative power of a single wish. The tone is warm, celebratory, and deeply human — rooted in real moments, real families, and the quiet conviction that showing up matters.
Goals & Objectives
  • Drive awareness of the milestone and reignite emotional connection to the Make-A-Wish mission among lapsed and prospective donors
  • Inspire sustainer conversion — move one-time givers to monthly giving by anchoring the ask to the ongoing impact of each wish
  • Leverage Cena's story as proof of radical commitment, positioning donors as co-heroes in the narrative alongside him
Target Audience
  • Existing mid-level donors (giving level $100–$999) with lapsed or declining recency — high reactivation potential
  • WWE and pop-culture audiences aged 30–55 with demonstrated affinity for cause-driven celebrity stories
  • Parents of young children — emotionally primed to the stakes of childhood illness and the value of joy
Creative Direction (3-Min Video)
Open on archival footage of early wish moments — raw, unpolished, real. Build through Cena's own words ("I just drop everything") interwoven with wish-kid interviews. Climax at the 650th wish reveal, scored for emotional release. Close with a direct, quiet ask: "One more wish starts with you." No celebrity gloss — this is a film about what showing up looks like over 20 years.
